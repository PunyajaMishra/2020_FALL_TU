Screenshot of my final mix in Reaper

I downloaded a very famous and (I think) a very liked politician's Speech about how they have plans of doing various worls to boost the economy in the worst times. I edited it to give a different meaning...how nothing needs to be done to boost the economy. Some upon hearing the speech wil recognise who the politician is andbe like, why do them like that, they be so nice! But, I was having fun editing in Reaper. So, I pasted the speech onto the first track and then started slicing, cutting and pasting the speech into something different 1 minute. After that from the Loops folder, I chose the drums track and pasted it with Tempo Match On to the second track. Here I just stretched it out to cover the entire speech. This gives it a little jazz. Finally, in the last track I added an aaplause at the very end and a girl laughing sound towards the starting when the poiltician's speech is saying somehting funny.. Finally I added Reaverb to the drums track to make it sound like a concert. This is my lab 10 Reaper editing.
